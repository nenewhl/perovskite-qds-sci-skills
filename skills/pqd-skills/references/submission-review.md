# PQD Submission and Review

## Journal Targeting

Select journals by claim type:

- Broad conceptual advance: Nature, Science, Nature Materials, Nature Nanotechnology, Nature Photonics, Nature Energy, Nature Communications, Science Advances.
- Energy/device advance: Joule, Energy & Environmental Science, Nature Energy, Advanced Energy Materials, ACS Energy Letters.
- Materials chemistry and nanoscience: Advanced Materials, JACS, Angewandte Chemie, Chemistry of Materials, Nano Letters, ACS Nano, Matter.
- Applied detectors/scintillators: Nature Photonics, Advanced Materials, ACS Nano, Advanced Functional Materials, Matter, relevant detector/imaging journals.

Before final advice, verify the current scope, article type, formatting, reference limits, data policies, and submission requirements from official journal pages.

## Presubmission Readiness

Check:

- One-sentence claim is specific and non-generic.
- Closest prior art is fairly represented.
- Evidence chain contains independent support for mechanism.
- Device metrics include statistics, not only champion values.
- Stability tests match the practical claim.
- Data availability and raw-data traceability are ready.
- Figures are readable at journal width.
- Supplementary information contains controls reviewers will request.

## Reviewer Simulation

Evaluate as three reviewers:

- Reviewer 1: materials chemistry and structure/composition.
- Reviewer 2: photophysics/mechanism.
- Reviewer 3: device/application and benchmarking.

For each reviewer, list:

- Summary of perceived claim.
- Major concerns.
- Required controls or analyses.
- Minor presentation issues.
- Likely recommendation and confidence.

## Response Strategy

- Start each response with a direct answer, then evidence, then manuscript change.
- Use respectful, concise language.
- When accepting criticism, state exactly where the manuscript was revised.
- When disagreeing, acknowledge the concern and provide data or literature support.
- Add new experiments only if they close a real logic gap.
- Do not hide weak points; bound the claim instead.

## Common PQD Reviewer Requests

- More batch statistics for devices or PLQY.
- Direct evidence for passivation or defect suppression.
- Long-term and operational stability under realistic conditions.
- Comparison with state-of-the-art using matched metrics.
- Clarification of ligand exchange and film formation.
- Separation of solution-phase and film/device behavior.
- X-ray dose calibration and detection-limit calculation.
