# PQD Workflow Core

## Intake

Before acting, identify:

- Material system: CsPbX3, FAPbX3, MAPbX3, mixed halide, 2D/3D, doped, alloyed, lead-free, core/shell, embedded glass/polymer/matrix, or heterostructure.
- Research axis: synthesis, surface/ligand chemistry, photophysics, stability, solar cell, LED/lasing, scintillation, X-ray detector, imaging, or theory.
- Evidence type: spectra, kinetics, structural characterization, microscopy, device metrics, statistical batches, literature notes, manuscript text, or review comments.
- Target output: PhD thesis chapter, SCI manuscript, Nature-style figure, graphical abstract, rebuttal, peer review, or data-analysis script.
- Audience: thesis committee, specialist reviewers, broad high-impact journal reviewers, or internal group meeting.

## Integrity Rules

- Never smooth, baseline-correct, normalize, or omit points without recording the operation.
- Never compare samples if acquisition settings, normalization basis, device area, film thickness, dose rate, or excitation conditions are incompatible.
- Never claim mechanism from a single correlation. Require controls, time evolution, spectroscopy, structural evidence, or literature precedent.
- Distinguish measured, calculated, fitted, inferred, and literature-reported quantities.
- For any numeric claim, preserve units, uncertainty, sample count, and fitting model.

## Preferred Work Order

1. Define the central scientific claim in one sentence.
2. Map each figure panel, dataset, or paragraph to one piece of evidence for that claim.
3. Audit data comparability and missing controls.
4. Process raw data with reversible steps and logged parameters.
5. Build benchmark tables against the closest literature, not only record values.
6. Draft the Results logic before the Introduction.
7. Make figures after the evidence chain is fixed.
8. Run reviewer-risk checks before polishing language.

## Output Quality Gate

A deliverable is not ready until it answers:

- What is the claim?
- What data support it?
- What alternative explanation remains?
- What control reduces that alternative?
- What exact metric lets readers compare with prior PQD work?
- What would a skeptical reviewer ask first?
