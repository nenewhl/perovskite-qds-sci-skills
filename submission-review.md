# Submission and Review

## Journal targeting

Check current official journal scope and author instructions. Do not rely on memory for scope, formatting, fees, data policies, image requirements, or reference style.

## Presubmission checklist

- Clear novelty statement.
- Matched claims and evidence.
- Adequate controls.
- Reproducibility and error analysis.
- Figure resolution and editability.
- Complete methods and materials information.
- Proper citation of primary sources.
- Consistent terminology across manuscript, figures, and supporting information.

## Reviewer simulation

Evaluate:

- Is the main claim overextended?
- Are controls sufficient?
- Are alternative mechanisms considered?
- Are device metrics measured under standard conditions?
- Are stability tests meaningful and comparable?
- Are benchmark comparisons fair?

## Response strategy

- Thank the reviewer.
- State the revision clearly.
- Provide evidence or explain limitation.
- Avoid defensive language.
- Use exact manuscript locations when available.
